good morrow sir our lodgings standing bleak upon the sea shook as the earth did quake the very principals did seem to rend and all to topple pure surprise and fear made me to quit the house but i much marvel that your lordship having rich tire about you should at these early hours shake off the golden slumber of repose 'tis most strange nature should be so conversant with pain being thereto not compelled the heavens through you increase our wonder and sets up your fame forever most rare
